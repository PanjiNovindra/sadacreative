PRODUCTION PHASE

At SADA CREATIVE, we take a slightly different approach to Android mobile app development.

Most mobile app developers are just that – developers. They get an order, make an app and deliver it to the client. While it’s typically cheap, this simplistic workflow is rife with opportunities for miscommunications and gaps. Worse, there’s no guarantee that the finished product will align with the client’s strategic goals. It’s just an app. Nothing more.

SADA CREATIVE we takes things a step further. We offer holistic, end-to-end mobile app development solutions, starting with strategy and continuing through post-launch support, upgrades and maintenance. That means our goal isn’t just to make awesome app (though we certainly do that). Rather, we want to act as a true strategic partner, helping clients think through their budget, scope of work, marketing and mobile roadmap. Here, it’s not just about the app. It’s about your success. And here’s how we do it.

Step 1: Strategy

A great app starts with a great strategy. We start by fleshing out the user experience, preparing a competitive analysis, and outlining the dev strategy. Practice makes perfect and we’ve done this more than 200 times. We leverage that experience to craft a better strategy for you.

Step 2: Design

Next, our designers work with you to generate a Style Guide that documents the color scheme, style, and important UI elements of your app. Once approved, we translate that into full screen mockups so you can have final say over exactly what your app will look like.

Step 3: Development

Once the designs are finalized, our developers begin coding. Expect to hear from us. A lot. We frequently send prototypes at key points in the development process so you can keep up with our progress and begin gathering feedback from your target audience.

Step 4: Testing & App Store Submission

We follow best testing practices throughout development to ensure the highest quality possible but QA doesn’t stop there. Our in-house testing team conducts further checks and balances post-production to fully check the app’s functionality. Once the Release Candidate is ready we get it submitted to your own Developer Account and launched on the various App Stores.

Step 5: Ongoing Support & Optimization

We truly believe that great apps are built over time. That’s why every app we develop includes a 6 month bug warranty and 6 month subscription to our Support Program. That means end-user customer support, daily testing, quarterly analytics reviews, and more. Why give that away? Because there’s no better way to show you that we’re in this for the long haul!
